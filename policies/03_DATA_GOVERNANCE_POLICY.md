# DATA GOVERNANCE POLICY

**Policy ID:** POL-DATA-001  
**Effective Date:** February 2026  
**Classification:** Controlled  
**Review Cycle:** Annual

---

## 1. POLICY STATEMENT

Data is a strategic asset. This policy establishes standards for data collection, usage, quality, privacy, and stewardship.

---

## 2. SCOPE

- All data created, collected, or managed by the organization
- All data pipeline stages (collection → processing → analysis → retention → disposal)
- All systems handling organizational data

---

## 3. DATA CLASSIFICATION

### Classification Levels

| Level | Definition | Handling Requirements |
|-------|-----------|----------------------|
| **Public** | Can be shared publicly | Standard retention policy |
| **Internal** | For internal use only | Access restricted to employees |
| **Confidential** | Sensitive business data | Encrypted, limited access, audit logging |
| **Restricted** | Personal/regulated data | Maximum protection, encryption, compliance tracking |

### Data Ownership
- Data owner: Responsible business unit
- Data steward: Technical maintenance and quality
- Data custodian: Security and compliance enforcement

---

## 4. KEY REQUIREMENTS

### 4.1 Data Quality
- Data accuracy verified annually
- Data completeness monitored
- Data consistency standards enforced
- Data lineage documented

### 4.2 Data Privacy
- Privacy impact assessments required for new systems
- Consent management for personal data
- Right to deletion (GDPR) implemented
- Data minimization principle applied

### 4.3 Data Retention
- Retention schedule by data type
- Justified rationale for retention periods
- Secure deletion procedures
- Archival for compliance

### 4.4 Data Access
- Principle of least privilege
- Access logging and monitoring
- Quarterly access reviews
- Separation of duties enforced

---

## 5. COMPLIANCE & REPORTING

- Monthly data governance status reports
- Quarterly compliance audits
- Annual data inventory review
- Incident reporting within 24 hours
