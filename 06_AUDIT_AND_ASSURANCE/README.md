## ğŸ” Audit Independence

Per ISO 19011:2018:
- Auditors must be independent from audited functions
- Reporting line to governance authority (not operational)
- Cannot audit own area of responsibility
- Maintain confidentiality of audit information
- Apply impartial professional judgment
